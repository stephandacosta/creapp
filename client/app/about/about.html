About us

CREapp is a free platform which allows:

  1. registered commercial real estate brokers to freely and directly publish purchase and lease requirements.
  2. purchasers and lessees to freely get in contact with registered commercial real estate brokers who can qualify and publish these requirements.
  3. sellers and lessors of commercial real estate to freely and efficiently search for purchase and lease requirements.

This platform is primarily meant to be used by commercial real estate brokers as an advertising tool to consolidate and publish purchase/lease requirements.
